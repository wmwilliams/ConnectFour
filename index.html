Introduction

Connect 4 is a classic board game that was published in 1974 by toy producer Hasbro. Its timeless simplicity made it one of my favorite games as a kid. Although at the time I had no idea, Connect 4 is a "solved" game, meaning if played correctly the first player should always be able to win.

Rules

The rules of the game are simple. Players take turns dropping their game piece into a selected column, and the first person to get 4 in a row wins. You can connect your pieces either vertically, horizontally, or even diagonally.
